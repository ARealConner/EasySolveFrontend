About EasySolve.AI

EasySolve.AI is an advanced calculator designed to automatically solve physics problems. Born from a study at WPI and built by a passionate team of undergraduates, our goal is to simplify complex physics calculations for students and professionals alike.

Features

  • Advanced calculator capable of solving a vast array of problems
  • Real-time computation as the user types.
  • Advanced AI Model capable of solving physics problems based on available information.
  • AI assistance throughout the solving process.
